# Claims boundary

Allowed:

- Band is the collaboration and witness surface. ScopeGate enforces authority-before-disclosure.
- The receipt and integrity gate show protected payload values were not posted to Band in this run; they do not replace an independent Band-side audit.
- RoomKey demonstrates authority-before-disclosure for a live Band agent room.

Forbidden class of claims:

- Do not say Band itself is the privacy layer.
- Do not say Band itself grants consent.
- Do not say Band itself stops replay.
- Do not say enforcement is guaranteed outside the receipt-proven run.
